## About WRO

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://github.com/DexterTaha/WRO-2024-FUTURE-ENGINEERS/assets/130682580/6d08f11e-2e99-4d6c-8994-0dc8783c6f05" width="1500">
</p>

The World Robot Olympiad (WRO) is an international robotics competition that challenges students to showcase their creativity and technical skills by designing and programming robots for various tasks.

The Future Engineers category focuses on developing innovative solutions to real-world problems using robotics and automation, fostering critical thinking and innovation among students.

## Our Robot: W15

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://github.com/DexterTaha/WRO-2024-FUTURE-ENGINEERS/assets/130682580/64664154-5967-491a-95b5-6b116392faf6" alt="W15" width="500">
</p>

Our robot, named W15, is a LEGO-based creation designed for agility and speed. It incorporates advanced steering, gearbox, sensing, and driving systems.

- **Aim**: Swift maneuvering through environments or parkour.
- **Objectives**: Detect and avoid obstacles, self-drive, auto-correct when in error.
- **Energy Source**: 9V adapter.
- **Intelligence**: Deep learning models, computer vision, Kalman filtering, and Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) concepts.
- **Competition Strategy**: Time attack race focus, adaptability to environment for speed.
